◆ SYS_BSR_PERIPH_ID

enum SYS_BSR_PERIPH_ID

BSR position.

Indicates the resource position on SW or HW BSR that can be reserved by a processing unit for use. Maximum supported peripheral ids are SYS_BSR_PERIPH_ID_MAX.
