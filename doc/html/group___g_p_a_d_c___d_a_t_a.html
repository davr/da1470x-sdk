◆ HW_GPADC_RESULT_MODE

enum HW_GPADC_RESULT_MODE

Sample mode controlling the LSB's of the stored result.

Enumerator
HW_GPADC_RESULT_EXTENDED 

Sample extension, the result is aligned on the MSBs. The lowest calculated LSB is extended over the unused bits

HW_GPADC_RESULT_TRUNCATED 

Sample truncation, the result is aligned on the 8 LSBs. Any additional accuracy isn't available

HW_GPADC_RESULT_NORMAL 

Normal mode, the result is aligned on the MSBs. Any unused LSBs are kept zero

HW_GPADC_RESULT_MODE_INVALID 

N.A.
